Information handled by this site

CYV Hub Scripts is a static website. The access key utility generates keys in your browser for the current visit. The generated key is not submitted to a CYV Hub server by this page. The page may store a local cooldown timestamp in your browser so it can limit key generation to once every 10 minutes.

The hosting provider may keep standard server logs such as IP address, requested URL, browser type, referrer, and request time for security, abuse prevention, and diagnostics.

Discord and external links

If you open the Discord support link, Discord handles your visit under its own policies. Do not send passwords, recovery codes, private cookies, payment information, or other sensitive account details through support messages.

Advertising disclosure

Ad serving is disabled during site review. If advertising is enabled later, Google AdSense or another ad provider may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver, measure, and limit ads. Ads should be placed around publisher content and should not be required to generate a key.
